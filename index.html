Comprehensive Sponsorship Impact Analysis 2024-2025

Study Methodology

Research Overview

The Abu Dhabi sponsorship with the New York Knicks was announced in October 2024. This dashboard presents the results of a comprehensive impact assessment study.

Survey Timing

  • Pre-sponsorship Survey: Conducted before the announcement in October 2024
  • Post-sponsorship Survey: Conducted in March 2025
  • Measurement Period: Approximately 5 months between surveys

Sample Characteristics

  • Sample Size: 1,000 respondents for each survey wave
  • Regions Covered: New York and New Jersey
  • Cities Included: Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, Staten Island, Long Island, Newark, and Jersey City

Analysis Approach

  • Comparative Analysis: Key metrics in pre and post-sponsorship results were compared
  • Segmentation: Analysis conducted between NY Knicks fans and non-fans
  • Engagement Tracking: Multiple touchpoints measured across awareness and engagement metrics

How, Where & When NY Knicks Fans Engage

Fan Behavior Insights

Understanding how NY Knicks fans consume basketball content and engage with sponsors provides valuable insights for optimizing sponsorship activations. The data reveals distinct viewing patterns and engagement channels that characterize the fan base.

Viewing Preferences

  • Watch Regardless of Teams: 37% of Knicks fans watch basketball matches regardless of which team is playing
  • Watch Multiple Teams: 42% watch matches of all teams they follow
  • Team-Specific Viewing: Only 21% exclusively watch NY Knicks matches

Viewing Frequency

  • Daily Viewers: 16% of Knicks fans watch basketball every day
  • 4-5 Times Weekly: 30% watch multiple times per week
  • 2-3 Times Weekly: 38% watch a few times per week
  • Once Weekly or Less: 16% watch less frequently

Viewing Locations

  • TV at Home/Friend's Place: 87% of Knicks fans
  • Sports Bar/Cafe: 51% of Knicks fans
  • In-Stadium (Live): 41% of Knicks fans
  • Mobile/Laptop/Tablet: 41% of Knicks fans
  • Radio/Audio Commentary: 19% of Knicks fans

Likelihood of Travelling Overseas for Pre-Season Matches

  • Travel Interest: 30% of NY Knicks fans expressed likelihood to travel overseas for pre-season matches
  • In-Stadium Connection: Fans who attend games in-person (41%) show higher propensity to travel to Abu Dhabi
  • Opportunity: Target the 41% of in-stadium attendees with exclusive travel packages and experiences

Top Awareness Channels

  • Social Media Posts: 68% (primary awareness driver)
  • In-stadium Signage/Screens: 36% (strong physical presence)
  • Team Merchandise: 34% (consistent visibility)
  • TV Ads: 26% (moderate impact)
  • Family/Friends: 19% (word-of-mouth)

Favorite Players

  • Jalen Brunson: 32% (top player)
  • Karl Antony Towns: 10%
  • Julius Randle: 9%
  • Mikal Bridges & Mitchell Robinson: 7% each
  • Cameron Payne: 6%
Fan Engagement Strategy

Analysis of fan behavior reveals four key strategic opportunities for Abu Dhabi's sponsorship activation. First, the broad viewing pattern (79% watch beyond just Knicks games) suggests expanding visibility across general basketball content rather than limiting to Knicks-specific channels. Second, the high engagement frequency (46% watching 4+ times weekly) and multi-environment consumption (87% at home, 51% at sports bars, 41% in-stadium) demands a segmented approach with tailored messaging for each viewing context—immersive experiences for in-stadium fans, social viewing activations for sports bar audiences, and second-screen engagement for at-home viewers. Third, the significant travel interest (30% overall, rising to 41% among in-stadium attendees) presents a direct conversion pathway through targeted travel packages for the most engaged segment. Finally, the channel effectiveness data points to a clear prioritization strategy: dominate social media (68% awareness), enhance in-stadium presence (36%), leverage team merchandise collaborations (34%), and utilize Jalen Brunson (32% fan favorite) as the primary ambassador to create authentic connections across all touchpoints.

Optimization Opportunities

Word-of-Mouth Activation: The gap between fans who learn about the sponsorship through word-of-mouth (19%) and those who generate new conversations (13%) highlights an opportunity to transform passive awareness into active advocacy. By creating content that resonates with the 41% of fans who attend games in-stadium and the 51% who watch at sports bars, Abu Dhabi can inspire more organic conversations.

Digital-to-Physical Conversion: The significant difference between digital discovery (62% Google searches) and word-of-mouth sharing (13%) indicates that fans are researching but not discussing Abu Dhabi. Connecting digital engagement to the 30% of fans expressing travel interest could create natural conversation triggers, particularly among the 32% who already visit the Experience Abu Dhabi website.

Key Insights & Recommendations

Substantial Sponsorship Awareness Achievement

46% of NY Knicks fans recognize Abu Dhabi as a team sponsor. More than half of those aware of Knicks sponsorships can identify Abu Dhabi among them. This represents a significant brand association milestone in a competitive sponsorship landscape.

Word-of-Mouth Opportunity

19% of fans learn about the sponsorship through conversations with family/friends, while 13% generate new conversations about Abu Dhabi after exposure. This represents a significant opportunity to transform passive awareness into active advocacy and create a self-reinforcing awareness cycle.

Key Insights

Digital-First Discovery: Fans are taking meaningful follow-up actions after becoming aware of the sponsorship, with digital exploration (62% Google searches, 51% social media) being the primary engagement pathway.

Authentic Integration Value: The effectiveness hierarchy reveals fans are discovering Abu Dhabi primarily through digital and experiential channels rather than traditional media, suggesting they value authentic integration into their basketball experience.

Engagement Opportunity: The sponsorship is successfully driving curiosity and digital exploration, with opportunity to further convert this interest into deeper engagement and advocacy.

Strategic Channel Investment Prioritization

Based on the channel effectiveness analysis, a strategic reallocation of resources will maximize impact and engagement.

Social Media Content Strategy

Capitalize on the dominant 68% awareness through social media with targeted content:

  • Create time-sensitive content aligned with game schedules - post highlight reels featuring Abu Dhabi branding immediately after games when engagement peaks
  • Develop interactive polls about Abu Dhabi facts during pre-game buildup to drive curiosity and learning
  • Create "Abu Dhabi Moment of the Game" features that showcase both basketball excellence and destination appeal
  • Implement targeted retargeting campaigns for the 32% who visited the Experience Abu Dhabi website

Merchandise Innovation

Leverage the 34% awareness through team merchandise with conversation-starting items:

  • Develop limited-edition co-branded items featuring Abu Dhabi - consider Knicks jerseys with Arabic calligraphy elements
  • Create collectible series featuring Knicks players at iconic Abu Dhabi landmarks
  • Design premium travel accessories (luggage tags, passport holders) that connect basketball fandom with travel aspiration
  • Implement QR codes on merchandise that unlock exclusive Abu Dhabi digital content

Word-of-Mouth Activation

Transform passive awareness into active advocacy:

  • Create interactive photo opportunities with Abu Dhabi-themed backdrops at games, featuring augmented reality elements that transport fans virtually to iconic locations
  • Develop "Basketball Cultural Exchange" viewing experiences at family-friendly restaurants and community centers featuring Middle Eastern cuisine, cultural demonstrations, and basketball activities
  • Partner with Jalen Brunson (32% fan favorite) for authentic content series exploring Abu Dhabi's sports facilities, cultural attractions, and hospitality
  • Create shareable content highlighting unexpected Abu Dhabi experiences that challenge perceptions and prompt "Did you know?" conversations

Viewing Experience Enhancement

Tailor content to specific viewing behaviors:

  • For Dedicated Basketball Viewers (37% watch regardless of teams): Create "Beyond the Game" content series showcasing Abu Dhabi's emerging basketball culture, facilities, and development programs
  • For Mobile/Digital Viewers (41%): Develop an interactive second-screen experience called "Abu Dhabi Basketball Journey" with real-time trivia during games, 360° tours, and augmented reality features
  • For Home Viewers: Create immersive "Game Night Experience" packages delivered to season ticket holders' homes with Abu Dhabi-inspired game day food recipes, themed decorations, and exclusive digital content

Lower Funnel Opportunity

Convert the 30% of fans expressing travel interest into actual visitors:

  • Premium In-Stadium Experience Package: Target the 41% of in-stadium attendees with exclusive packages including courtside seats for Abu Dhabi games, player meet-and-greets, and behind-the-scenes access
  • Family Package: Design multi-generational experiences targeting the 87% who watch at home with family-friendly activities beyond basketball
  • Limited Availability Messaging: Create urgency with countdown timers and limited slot availability for special experiences
  • Digital Sweepstakes: Implement social media contests for all-expenses-paid trips to Abu Dhabi games
  • On-Site VIP Treatment: Ensure exceptional experiences for travelers to generate word-of-mouth
  • Post-Trip Advocacy Program: Create incentives for travelers to share experiences and become ambassadors
Strategic Implementation Priorities

Short-Term Focus: Enhance social media content strategy and in-stadium activations to capitalize on the strongest awareness channels. Redirect resources from underperforming channels (news articles, broadcast ads, fan forums) to these high-impact touchpoints.

Medium-Term Development: Launch the merchandise innovation program and second-screen experience to deepen engagement with the most active fan segments.

Long-Term Vision: Create a year-round content calendar aligned with basketball seasons and events, develop loyalty programs that reward continued engagement with Abu Dhabi content, and establish Abu Dhabi as an authentic part of the Knicks fan experience.
